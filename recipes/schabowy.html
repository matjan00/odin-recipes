Schabowy - Polish cutlet

Schabowy, or rather Kotlet schabowy as is it's proper Polish name is a pork cutlet similar to the German Schnitzel

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • an egg
  • boneless pork chop
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps:

  1. Pound the meat to flaten it.
  2. Pour flour onto a plate.
  3. Whisk an egg in a bowl.
  4. Heat oil on a pan.
  5. Dip the meat in the egg and then in the flour.
  6. Cook for around 5 minutes on each side.
