Zurek

Zurek - traditional Polish soup

Zurek is a pretty unique soup. Its' sour flavour can be weird at first, but the longer you eat it, the better it gets.

Ingredients:

  • meat stock
  • an egg
  • white sausage
  • white onion
  • potatoes
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic
  • dried marjoram
  • whipped cream
  • parsley roots
  • Sour Rye Flour Starter

Steps:

  1. Heat the stock
  2. Fry the sausage, onion and garlic.
  3. Add all solid ingredients to the stock.
  4. Let it boil for 30-40 minuts.
  5. Add Sour Rye Flour Starter and boil for another 10 minutes.
  6. Make sure to taste it as you go to adjust the level of sourness.
