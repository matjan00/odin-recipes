Pierogi

Pierogi - Polish dumplings

Pierogi are the Eastern European version of dumplings. They come in a wide range of fillings from potato-and-cheese to meat or even various fruit-filled ones.

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • an egg
  • butter
  • cottage cheese
  • potatoes
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil potatoes.
  2. Drain them
  3. Mash them and mix with cottage cheese, salt and pepper.
  4. Cool the mixture.
  5. Make the dumpling dough.
  6. Fill the Pierogi with the filling.
  7. Boil for 5 minutes in a boiling water.
